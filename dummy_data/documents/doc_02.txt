PROJECT CHARTER: PROJECT AURA-KG
System Overview & Scope Document

1. OVERVIEW & DOMAIN
Project AURA-KG is an internal initiative dedicated to the domain of Personal knowledge management and applied NLP. The goal is to turn unstructured team communications (emails, chats, notes) into a queryable knowledge graph.

2. CORE TEAM & ROLES
- Varun: Data pipeline & ingestion
- Tejus: NLP & graph construction
- Aniketh: LLM & retrieval
- Nikshith: Frontend & visualization

3. TECHNOLOGY STACK (TOOLS IN USE)
The platform is built on the following stack: Python, Neo4j, spaCy, Transformers, FAISS, Streamlit, and Cytoscape.js. 

4. PRIMARY OUTCOME
The ultimate outcome of this project is to eliminate institutional developer amnesia by allowing team members to query historical technical decisions through a natural language interface.
